## Notes

- These are affiliate links from the video (6 years old) -- some products may be unavailable or prices changed. Check each link.
- The video is 6 years old so some exact products may be discontinued. If a link is dead, search for the same specs.
- **Power supply sizing**: TEC1-12709 draws up to 9A. Pump ~3A. Fan ~1A. Total ~13-14A at 12V. Make sure PSU can handle it.
- **CPU Cooler sits on the HOT side** of the Peltier. Water block sits on the COLD side.
- **CRITICAL**: Never power the Peltier without cooling attached -- destroys itself in seconds.
- **Cold side identification**: Apply 12V for 2 seconds max. Usually the side with lettering.
- **Tubing**: Use opaque/dark tubing to prevent algae growth.
